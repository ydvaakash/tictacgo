TicTacGo

Game Instructions

  1. You are 'Player 1' and the computer is 'Player 2'.
  2. Use either 'W', 'A', 'S', 'D' or the arrow keys on your keyboard to move on the board.
  3. Press 'Space' to mark your symbol in the chosen box.
  4. You can also use your mouse or trackpad to play the game.
